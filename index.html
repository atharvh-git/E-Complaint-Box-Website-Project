About Project:

E-Complaint Box is preferably created for the complaints that students have regarding our college. Here, to submit a complaint, he/she will have to fill up a form given above which takes some basic information about him/her, subject of complaint, some details of complaint, and a confirmation from the complainee that he/she is not submitting any false accusation regarding the college.

The filled form with all sequencial details will be submitted to the complaint-solving authority, for e.g. The Students' Council of our college, which will get this data on their Email ID, and will be able to take action accordingly.

Here, in this project development, we used three languages which are primarily used in front-end project development which are HTML(HyperText Markup Language), CSS(Cascading Style Sheets) and JavaScript(JS).
